### When Does Enterprise Make Sense?

Enterprise is better than Pro when you need:
- **More than 10K agents per debate** (Pro caps at 10K)
- **1M-agent capability** (Enterprise only)
- **White-label, custom agents, or SLA guarantees**

For volume alone, Pro is more cost-effective up to ~225 debates/month ($200), where Enterprise's 100K agents per debate provides better value.

## Why Hybrid Pricing?

**Old model:** "Unlimited debates" meant we absorbed unpredictable costs.

**New model:** A generous base covers typical usage. Overages are transparent and low. You only pay more if you use more.

- Pro at 20 debates/month: most teams never hit overage
- Enterprise at 25 debates/month: heavy users get predictable base costs
- 1M agents: available on-demand without a higher subscription tier
