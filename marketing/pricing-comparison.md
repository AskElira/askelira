## Scenario Examples

### Startup (5-person engineering team)
- ~15 debates/month for architecture and tech decisions
- 10K agents is sufficient
- **Best plan: Pro at $20/month**

### Growth-stage company
- ~40 debates/month across teams
- Occasional 100K-agent debates for critical decisions
- **Best plan: Enterprise at $200/month** (25 included + 15 overages = $320/month)

### Trading firm
- ~30 debates/month for market analysis
- Weekly 1M-agent debates for major position decisions (~4/month)
- **Best plan: Enterprise at $200 + 5 overages ($40) + 4 x $80 (1M) = $560/month**

### Solo developer
- ~5 debates/month
- Self-hosting is easy for you
- **Best plan: Self-Hosted at ~$0.35/month** (API costs only)

### Enterprise org (50+ engineers)
- 100+ debates/month
- Need SLA, white-label, on-premise
- **Best plan: Enterprise at $200 + 75 overages = $800/month**
- That's $8/decision for enterprise-grade swarm intelligence

## Self-Hosted vs Managed: When to Choose

| Choose Self-Hosted if... | Choose Managed if... |
|---|---|
| You want zero monthly cost | You want zero setup |
| You already manage infrastructure | You don't want to manage servers |
| Data must stay on your servers | Convenience matters more than cost |
| You enjoy tinkering | You want guaranteed uptime (SLA) |
| Your usage is highly variable | You want email/dedicated support |
| You need custom modifications | You need white-label or custom agents |

## FAQ

**Q: What happens if I exceed my included debates?**
A: You're automatically charged the overage rate. No service interruption.

**Q: Do unused debates roll over?**
A: Pro rolls over up to 40. Enterprise rolls over up to 50. Free tier resets monthly.

**Q: Can I mix 100K and 1M agent debates on Enterprise?**
A: Yes. Your 25 included debates work with up to 100K agents. 1M-agent debates are billed separately at $80 each.

**Q: Is there an annual discount?**
A: Contact sales for annual pricing. Typical discount is 2 months free.

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**
A: Yes. Pro includes a 7-day free trial. Enterprise includes a custom pilot program.
