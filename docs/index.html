The 4-Phase Pipeline

Every question flows through four specialized AI agents

1

Alba Research

Queries Brave Search, synthesizes findings across thousands of sources, identifies key decision factors and context.

↓
2

David Debate

Orchestrates thousands of agents in a structured debate. Pro vs. con arguments are weighted, scored, and ranked.

↓
3

Vex Audit

Scans debate results for cognitive biases, logical fallacies, missing perspectives, and unsupported claims.

↓
4

Elira Synthesis

Produces the final decision with confidence score, key arguments for and against, risk factors, and next steps.
